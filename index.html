TECH WEEK 2017
01-09 APRIL

ABOUT

Tech Week 2017, the third edition of this technical extravaganza, is back! And this time, it's bigger and better! Organised by IEEE NSIT and E.volve(The Software development society of NSIT), Tech Week’17 aims to host a week long series of technical events, competitions and many fun activities. With an aim to carry forward the rich coding culture of NSIT, the Tech Week promises to challenge the brightest of minds, while at the same time, being a fun learning experience. The Tech Week 2017 is scheduled for 1st to 9th April, 2017 at Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology. It will feature a plethora of innovative events and competitions, including 2 overnight hackathons , which would surely test the technical knowledge of the participants in diverse fields such as hardware prototyping, software development and analog design to develop applications, prototypes and designs, while at the same time challenging their creative flair to come up with unique ideas. The aim of Tech Week 2017 is to provide a conducive environment to students from all over India to collaborate, hone their skills and compete with the best talent of the country. This will be the perfect platform for students to network, collectively ideate and develop apps making use of the latest cutting edge technologies.

ORGANISERS

IEEE NSIT

IEEE NSIT, one of the most active Student Branches in India, aims to further the motto of IEEE i.e. to simulate young minds and create an environment of technical excellence for them. With its regularly organised SIGs, national conferences, educative meets and seminars, IEEE NSIT strives towards helping students learn new skills and apply them in the real world. IEEE NSIT aims to provide students the perfect platform to jumpstart their professional career. Our Special Interest Groups(SIGs) cater to a variety of interests such as C,C++, Robotics, Electronics, Arduino, etc. IEEE NSIT has conducted workshops on The Game Development, Web Development, Ethical Hacking, Machine Learning and AndroidApp Development to name just a few. Also, under IEEE's Mentorship Program, students get a wonderful opportunity to apply their practical skills and collaborate on projects with their seniors. A major highlight of IEEE are the regularly organised conferences, which are a brilliant platform for students to learn from the industry's top experts, showcase their skills and build an excellent network. A prime example of this was the All India Computer Society Student –Young Professional Congress (AICSSYC 2015), organized in December 2015 by IEEE NSIT. This was an international event with the attendance of delegates from IEEE International and leaders of India’s industry and technology fields. It witnessed the participation of over 200 delegates from different sections of India.

e.volve

E.volve is the Software Development Society of NSIT, which aims to carry forward the rich coding culture of the college. It provides the perfect opportunity to budding programmers in the college to hone their skills, collaborate as well as compete with the best talent of the country. The main aim of E.volve is to provide a conducive environment to the coding enthusiasts and is the rallying point for all the top-notch developers of NSIT. Evolve is the baton holder of all software hackathons, tech talks and open-source development events at NSIT. Since the establishment of this society, NSIT has witnessed an ever more increase in the culture of software development, reaching the paradigms of web-development and open-source contributions. Evolve was the organising society for Hack@NSIT 2016 and Androidathon 2015.

Hardware Hackathon

  • The Hardware Hackathon promises to challenge the technical skills and creative minds of the participants.
  • This will be an overnight hardware prototyping event on advancing technologies such as the Internet of Things, Intelligent Appliances, etc.
  • Teams from all over India will be shortlisted on the basis of the originality, creativity, practicality and ingenuity of their idea.
  • This is a two day event, to be held on 31 March and 1 April.
Register

Hack@NSIT 2017

  • The software counterpart of the Hardware Hackathon, Hack@NSIT2017 provides the perfect platform to budding developers to show off their skills.
  • The competition aims to challenge participants to apply their technical knowledge to develop an application relevant to the themes of the event in the time span of 24 hours
  • The event will be held at the end of the Tech Week , i.e. 7 and 8 April.
Register

TI Analog Maker

IEEE NSIT in partnership with Texas Instruments(TI) organises the Texas Instruments Analog Maker competition for the undergraduate and postgraduate students of NSIT. Keeping in tradition with TI's philosophy of providing the tools necessary to inspire innovation and taking engineering concepts from the book to the breadboard, the event focuses on using TI-Analog IC's in the best way to solve system level design problems.The focus will be on Linear Integrated Circuits and their use in system level design.

Online Coding Competition

An online competitive coding competition will allow participants from all over India to compete from their homes and hostels throughout the week, to solve complex coding problems.

Fun Events

The entire week will see a lot of small fun events to keep the participants and students engaged and entertained.
